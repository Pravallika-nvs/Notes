3. Importance of Pressure Drop

Pressure drop is a crucial aspect in fluid mechanics, especially in the design and operation of piping systems.

It is the difference in pressure between two points in a fluid system.

Understanding pressure drop helps in:

  • Designing efficient piping systems
  • Calculating pump requirements
  • Ensuring safety and reliability

Bernoulli’s Equation helps understand this concept. It is based on Newton's Second Law of Motion, F = ma.
