1. What is a Fluid?

A fluid is a substance that does not have a definite shape but takes the shape of its container. Examples: Water and vapour are both fluids.

Characteristics of a Fluid

  • Fluids can flow and take the shape of their containers.
  • Fluids can be compressed or expanded.
  • Fluids exert pressure in all directions.
  • Fluids undergo continuous deformation when any shear force is applied to them.
