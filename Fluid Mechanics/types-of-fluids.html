2. Types of Fluids

Fluids can be classified into two main types: Ideal Fluids and Real Fluids

2.1 Ideal Fluids

An ideal fluid is a theoretical concept, used as a benchmark.

It has:

  • No viscosity
  • No internal resistance

It is imaginary – such fluids do not exist in reality.

2.2 Real Fluids

A real fluid exists in the real world.

It has:

  • Viscosity
  • Internal resistance

Examples: Water, oil, air, etc.
