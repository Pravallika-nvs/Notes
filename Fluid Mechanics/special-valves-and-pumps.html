8. Special Valves and Pumps

Special valves and pumps are designed for specific applications in fluid systems.

8.1 Diaphragm Valves

  • Used for highly corrosive fluids.
  • Made from rubber or plastic to avoid corrosion.

8.2 Compressor and Blowers

  • Used for gases, similar to liquid pumps.
  • Impellers rotate at high speeds to compress or move gas.
  • Includes:
    • Fans
    • Centrifugal Blowers
    • Compressors
